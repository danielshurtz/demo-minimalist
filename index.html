Our Approach

Subtract until
only truth remains.

We source from a handful of farmers, fishers, and foragers we trust completely. The menu changes with what arrives at the door each morning — not with what's printed on a card.

Chef Yuki Tanaka trained under three Michelin-starred kitchens in Kyoto before moving to Manila. Her food is quiet but insistent. It stays with you.
